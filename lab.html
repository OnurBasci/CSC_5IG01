Lab Homework 4: Unity Roll-A-Ball in VR

This week I implemented the VR version of Roll-A-Ball. I haven't worked with Unity VR before so this was a new experience for me. I had a little bit difficulties to associate meta application with meta quest therefore I could not work in real time with Unity. I had to build the apk file and send it to the meta quest for each time I wanted to test the algorithm.

I started by creating the scene and setting the camera. I did not use the default Unity camera but I used OVRCameraRig. Then I exported my roll a boll project and imported it to my vr project. I decided to implement a direct selection method to be able to hold the roll a ball platform and move the ball by rotating my hand. To do that I added a sphere collider to right and left hand controller. I also adjusted the layers in a way that the platform only detects collision with hand devices. And here is the logic of my selection technique. Finally I build the final project and I was able to select and rotate the platform with the hand devices. I have to say that the game became much more interesting!
